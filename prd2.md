CORE FUNTIONS FOR DEEPHYSIO CLINIC MANAGEMENT APPLICATION
Core Functional Requirements
1. User & Role Management
• Admin, therapist, receptionist, billing roles.
• Permissions to restrict access to sensitive data.
• Audit trail of user actions and login tracking.
Practice Administration
2. Appointment & Scheduling
• Daily/weekly/monthly calendar view.
• Drag-and-drop booking interface.
• Patient waitlist and availability blocks.
• Online booking widget for patients.
• Automatic reminders (email + SMS). 
Client & Clinical Records
3. Client Management (CRM)
• Comprehensive patient profiles: contact details, referral source, insurance 
info, emergency contacts.
• History of visits, treatments, exercises prescribed.
4. Clinical Notes & Templates
• Library of customizable note templates for assessment, treatment, progress 
notes.
• Ability to add annotations, attachments, images.
• Template library you can manage (add/edit/delete).
• Save and recall past patient notes. 
Communication & Templates
5. Email, SMS & Telehealth Communication
• Bulk and one-to-one email and SMS messaging.
• Automated appointment reminders (configurable timing).
• Two-way SMS chat with patients.
• Integration with video calls (Zoom / telehealth). 
Templates to include:
• Appointment confirmation
• Appointment reminder
• Cancellation notification
• Follow-up message
• Telehealth link message
• General news/updates template
Each template should be editable by the admin and support variables (e.g., patient 
name, appointment time).
Billing & Payments
6. Invoices & Payments
• Generate invoices automatically after appointments.
• Send payment reminders and overdue notices via email/SMS.
• Track payment status.
• Payment gateway integration (Stripe, PayPal).
• Insurance claims support.
Invoice Template Fields
• Clinic details
• Patient details
• Services rendered
• Price breakdown
• Taxes
• Invoice number
• Payment terms and status
Online Forms & Intake
7. Intake Forms, Consent, Assessments
• Create and manage online forms that auto-populate patient records.
• Intake questionnaires, consent forms, health history forms.
• Custom form builder (drag/dynamic fields). 
Reporting & Clinic Analytics
8. Analytics & Insights
Provide dashboards and reports for:
• Appointment volume/trends
• Revenue & outstanding payments
• No-show and cancellation rates
• Patient growth over time
• Service popularity (e.g., types of treatment)
• Staff performance metrics
Reports should be exportable (CSV/PDF) and filterable by date range and clinic 
location. 
Clinic Growth & Marketing
9. Marketing & Engagement Tools
• Email marketing campaigns with templates (newsletters, promotions).
• Segment patient lists (e.g., by service, visit date, location).
• Automated campaigns for follow-ups (e.g., 6-month check-in).
• Track open and click-through rates on email campaigns.
Integrations
10. External System Integration
• Calendar sync with Google/Outlook.
• Payment processors.
• Accounting platforms (QuickBooks, Xero).
• Optional integrations with exercise prescription systems or physiotherapy 
tools. 
Security & Compliance
11. Security Requirements
• Secure login (two-factor optional).
• Data encryption at rest and in transit.
• Backup and restore procedures.
• Compliance with local health data standards (e.g., GDPR in UK/EU).
UX & UI Requirements
12. Ease of Use
• Clean, intuitive interface.
• Mobile responsive design.
• Fast onboarding with setup help and defaults.
Optional Enhancements
13. AI-Assisted Clinical Support
• AI tools to assist with clinical notes and documentation (optional addon). 
14. Multi-Clinic Support
• Manage multiple locations with consolidated dashboards.
