# DeePhysio Clinic SaaS – Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Product Name
DeePhysio Clinic Management System

## Goal
Build a modern SaaS platform for physiotherapy clinics to manage patients, appointments, clinical notes, billing, and communication in one centralized dashboard.

## Target Users
- Clinic Admin
- Therapists
- Receptionists
- Billing Staff

## Core Modules
1. **Dashboard**: Overview of clinic operations (Appointments, Revenue, Patients).
2. **Appointments**: Calendar scheduling, Waitlist, Availability.
3. **Patients (CRM)**: Patient list, Profiles, Visit History, Insurance.
4. **Clinical Notes**: Assessment/Treatment/Progress notes, Templates.
5. **Communication**: SMS, Email, Telehealth, Message Templates.
6. **Billing & Payments**: Invoices, Payments, Insurance Claims.
7. **Forms & Intake**: Online intake/consent forms, Form Builder.
8. **Analytics**: Revenue/Appointment/Growth reports.
9. **Marketing**: Email campaigns, Segmentation, Automation.
10. **Integrations**: Google/Outlook Calendar, Stripe, PayPal, QuickBooks, Xero.
11. **Settings**: User management, Roles, Clinic details, Multi-location.

## Tech Stack
- **Frontend**: React.js, TailwindCSS, Vite
- **Language**: JavaScript (ES6)

## UI Requirements
- Fully responsive (Mobile/Tablet/Desktop)
- Professional Medical/SaaS theme
- Custom shadow: `rgba(60, 64, 67, 0.3) 0px 1px 2px 0px, rgba(60, 64, 67, 0.15) 0px 1px 3px 1px`
